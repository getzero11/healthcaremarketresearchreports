## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Routine animal vaccination is often more affordable and also it reduces transmission of microorganisms in the animal population, and reduces animal suffering. In veterinary medicine, vaccination has also proven to be a boon for animal health. Diseases such as canine parvovirus and canine distemper, feline leukemia, or equine tetanus, have been nearly eliminated by vaccination.
