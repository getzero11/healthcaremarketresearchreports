## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Sterilization is a procedure to eliminate microbial organisms to achieve a sterile microbial environment. Disinfectants are antimicrobial agents that are applied to non-living objects to destroy those microbes living on the objects. Sterilization equipment and disinfectants are used in healthcare facilities such as hospitals, clinics and laboratories to kill or eliminate harmful microorganisms.

Sterilization is performed via heat sterilization equipment, low temperature sterilization equipment, filtration sterilization equipment and radiation sterilization equipment. Disinfectants that are utilized to clean medical devices and pharmaceutical products include low, intermediate and high level disinfectants, and oxidizing and non-oxidizing disinfection.
