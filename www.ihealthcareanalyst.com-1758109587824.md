Anti-aging products intended to make people more attractive are generally cosmetics, whereas some products are intended to affect the structure or function of the body, such as the skin, are medical devices.

Anti-aging products and services market is expected to be one of the fastest growing markets worldwide due to rising geriatric population, high surveillance of regulatory bodies and increasing awareness of aging signs in generation X and generation Y. The emerging economies of Asia and South America are likely to accelerate the anti-aging market during the forecast period.
