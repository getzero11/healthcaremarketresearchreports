## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Nephrology and urology devices are used for treatment of kidney and urinary system related problems such as chronic kidney disease (CKD), kidney stone, benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), urinary incontinence and pelvic organs prolapse.

Hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis (PD) are used for dialysis of kidney in patients with kidney failure. Hemodialysis is the most commonly used method for kidney dialysis. However, the market for peritoneal dialysis has observed a significant growth in the last two decades with the introduction of intermittent peritoneal dialysis (IPD), automated peritoneal dialysis and continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis (CAPD) procedures.
