## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Anti-infectives medicines prevent or treat infections, and include antibiotics and antibacterials, antifungals, antivirals and antiprotozoals or antiparasitic medications. The selection of an appropriate dose of an anti-infective agent is based on the site of infection, identity and known or presumed susceptibility of the infecting organism, dose-related drug toxicity, and patient’s ability to eliminate the drug.
