## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Electrophoresis is an analytical technique used in laboratories to separate macromolecules based on their size. Its principle is that the charged particles of a sample migrate in an applied electrical field. When conducted in a solution, samples are separated according to their surface charge.
