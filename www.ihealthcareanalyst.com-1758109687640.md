## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Cardiovascular monitoring and diagnostics generally refers to continuous monitoring of heart activity, generally by electrocardiography, with assessment of the patient’s condition relative to their cardiac rhythm.  Diagnosis and monitoring of cardiovascular disorders have become the need for the global healthcare industry in order to prevent the rapid rise in the deaths due to various cardiovascular disorders. Monitoring of the heart rate can be performed as part of electrocardiography, but it can also be measured conveniently with specific heart rate monitors. Globally rising aging population, incidence of cardiovascular diseases and growing awareness about the risks associated cardiovascular diseases primarily contribute towards the growth of global cardiovascular monitoring and diagnostic devices market.
