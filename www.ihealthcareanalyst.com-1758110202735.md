## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Allergy diagnostics and therapeutics play a crucial role in identifying and managing allergic conditions, improving patients’ health and quality of life. Diagnostics involve tools such as skin prick tests, blood tests for specific IgE antibodies, and advanced molecular diagnostics, which pinpoint the precise allergen causing the reaction. Recent innovations, such as biologic drugs targeting specific immune pathways, have transformed allergy treatment, offering hope for individuals with severe or refractory cases.
