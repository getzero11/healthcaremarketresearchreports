## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Personal mobility devices are designed to assist walking or otherwise improve the mobility of people with a mobility impairment. A wide range of new, improved, and innovative personal mobility devices are currently available in the global market. Technological advancements, improved product design, government support with reimbursement provisions, public awareness about new products, increased availability and adoption are some of the factors contributing to the growth of global personal mobility devices market.

The global personal mobility devices market report provides market size estimates ($million 2023 to 2033) for key market segmented by the type of device used (medical furniture; bathroom safety products such as patient lifts, bars and railings, commodes and shower chairs, medical beds; medical mobility aids &amp; ambulatory devices such as  manual and electric wheelchairs; canes &amp; crutches, scooters, stair lifts, and walkers, etc.), and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).
