## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Gelatin or collagen hydrolysate is an irreversibly hydrolyzed form of collagen used in many medical applications such as treating complications of the bones and skin, weight loss and for treating osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and brittle bones (osteoporosis) and for strengthening bones, joints, and fingernails. Gelatin is also used for improving hair quality and to shorten recovery after exercise and sports-related injuries.

The global gelatin market report estimates the market size ($million 2023 to 2033) and market volume (tones), for key market segmented by the source type (bovine, chicken, marine, porcine, etc.), application (bone and joint health, cosmeceuticals, food and beverages, nutraceuticals, pet food, pharmaceuticals, photography, etc.), and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).
