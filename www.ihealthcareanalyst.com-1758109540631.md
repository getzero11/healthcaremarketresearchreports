## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Ophthalmology diagnostics includes corneal imaging: topography (pentacam), specular microscopy, slit-lamp photography, and confocal microscopy, corneal analysis for infectious keratitis, electroretinography, fluorescein imaging, ocular infection diagnosis, ocular pathology, and ultrasound of the eye. The ophthalmology diagnostic devices assist the ophthalmologists to conduct detailed examination of the periphery and central areas of eyes and help in determination of ophthalmic disorders. Increasing demand for novel diagnostic technological platforms, efficient treatment methodologies, adoption by patient population considering rapid technological advances, and growth of the overall medical device industry are the major driving factors for the growth of global ophthalmology diagnostics and surgical devices market.
