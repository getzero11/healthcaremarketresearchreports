## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Electronic systems in medical equipment, devices and probes rely on sensor signals as a basis for control activities, accurate diagnosis and treatment. sensor is a device that measures a physical property and responds by converting it into an electrical or optical signal. Sensors are used in a wide array of healthcare applications such as in dialysis machines, insulin pumps, heart rate monitoring devices, pressure monitoring devices, measuring drug delivery rates, medical imaging devices, blood glucose monitoring, etc. The medical device sensors have been divided based on sensor type (physiology, air, temp, movement, pressure, image, force, etc.) and applications or measurement specialties (diagnostics, monitoring, surgical, imaging, interventional, and therapeutics). Increasing focus on developing wearable and implantable sensors with real time sensing of health parameters are likely to drive global medical sensors market.
