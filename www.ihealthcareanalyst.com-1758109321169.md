## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Prenatal, fetal and neonatal equipment are the devices that help in taking care of newly born children and babies developing inside mother’s womb. They also include devices that help monitor and diagnose both mother and the fetus. The recent statistics indicates that. more than one million newborns die of prematurity each year and those who are able to survive suffer from several disabilities.

High incidence rate of premature births, older age of maternal population, high birth rates in less developed and developing economies, and public awareness about prenatal and neonatal care are some the factors contributing to the growth of global prenatal, fetal and neonatal equipment market.
