## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Geriatric care management is a holistic, client-centered approach to caring for older adults or others facing ongoing health challenges. It is the process of planning and coordinating care of the elderly and others with physical and/or mental impairments to meet their long term care needs, improve their quality of life, and maintain their independence for as long as possible. This broad term encompasses such services as assisted living, adult day care, long term care, nursing homes (often referred to as residential care), hospice care, and home care. Twentieth century marks a change in demographic trends such as aging population which play a decisive role and have profound effect on economic growth and living standards. By 2020, aging is expected to witness a serious push due to large pool of post Second World War baby boomers moving into retirement. Major reasons for occurrence of global aging are increased longevity, efficient and affordable basic healthcare facilities around the globe.
