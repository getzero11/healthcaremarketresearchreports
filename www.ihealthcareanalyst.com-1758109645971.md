## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

A bag valve mask (BVM) or Ambu bag or generically as a manual resuscitator or “self-inflating bag”, is a hand-held device commonly used to provide positive pressure ventilation to patients who are not breathing or not breathing adequately. The device is a required part of resuscitation kits for trained professionals in out-of-hospital settings (such as ambulance crews) and is also frequently used in hospitals as part of standard equipment found on a crash cart, in emergency rooms or other critical care settings.

The global bag valve masks market report provides market size estimates ($million 2023 to 2033) for key market segmented by BVM disposable and reusable product types, and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).

Disposable BVMs and reusable BVMs are the two major types of BVMs available in the global market. The reusable BVM segment accounted for the largest share of revenue in the global BVM market.  High price of reusable BVMs compared to disposable BVMs and growing demand for reusable products in developing regions a there key governing factors for the largest share of the reusable BVM segment. The share of disposable bag valve masks is likely to decrease negligibly during the forecast period.

The global market for bag valve masks market research report is further divided by geography into North America (U.S., Canada), Europe (U.K., Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Rest of EU), Asia Pacific (Japan, China, India, Rest of APAC), Latin America (Brazil, Mexico, Rest of LA), and Rest of the World.

In terms of geography, North America region accounted for the largest share of the total bag valve masks market. Rising prevalence of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) among the population, increasing healthcare awareness regarding the availability of the treatment of wide range of chronic respiratory diseases and rising demand for technological advancements in bag valve masks to upgrade existing equipment are the major factors driving the global market.
