## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Chronic diseases, such as heart disease, cancer, and diabetes can be largely preventable or can be detected early through appropriate early screenings technologies (automated, personalized medicine, vaccination – DTP, HPV, meningococcal), chronic disease management (asthma, blood pressure, cardiovascular, and blood glucose monitoring) and advanced technologies (e-prescription, automated and smart technologies, clinical support systems).

Preventive healthcare technologies and services market consist of screening, monitoring, prophylaxis and healthcare information technology for disease prevention. The advanced technologies aid in prevention of diseases by predetermination of the disease or immunization. Preventive healthcare technologies and services are increasing in demand due to rising incidences of cardiovascular diseases, chronic respiratory diseases, dispensing or prescription errors, diabetes and certain infectious diseases.

The global preventive healthcare technology and services market is expected to perform better considering the supportive government policies, rising social awareness about prophylaxis and increasing preference for self-monitoring devices during the forecast period.
