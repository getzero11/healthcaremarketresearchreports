## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

A drug device combination product is composed of any combination of a drug and a device; a biological product and a device; a drug and a biological product; or a drug, device, and a biological product. Under 21 CFR 3.2 (e), a combination product is defined as a product comprised of two or more regulated components (i.e., drug/device, biologic/device, drug/biologic, or drug/device/biologic) that are physically, chemically, or otherwise combined or mixed and produced as a single entity; and, two or more separate products packaged together in a single package or as a unit and comprised of drug and device products, device and biological products, or biological and drug products.

The global drug device combination products market segmentation is based on product type \[drug eluting stents, (oronary stents, peripheral vascular stents), infusion pumps (implantable infusion pumps, ambulatory infusion pumps), photodynamic therapy devices (photosensitizers), orthopedic combination devices (antibiotic bone cement, bone graft implants), wound care combination devices (antibiotic wound care products), inhalers (nebulizers, metered dose inhalers, dry powder inhalers), transdermal patches, and other devices (intraocular implants, drug eluting beads)\].
