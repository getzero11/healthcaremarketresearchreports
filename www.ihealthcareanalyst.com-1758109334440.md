## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Anxiety disorders and depression are different, but people with depression often experience symptoms similar to those of an anxiety disorder, such as nervousness, irritability, and problems sleeping and concentrating. But each disorder has its own causes and its own emotional and behavioral symptoms. Anxiety disorders and depression are common psychiatric disorders that affect a large number of people during their lifetime. According to the WHO, by 2020, depression and anxiety disorders including stress related psychiatric conditions would rival cardiovascular diseases as the major health disorder with the highest disease burden. Currently, medications together with psychotherapy are the best treatment approach for patients suffering from depression and anxiety disorders.
