## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Consumer genomics, encompassing both direct-to-consumer applications genetic testing that is accessed by a consumer directly from a commercial company apart from a healthcare provider) and consumer-driven genetic testing (genetic testing ordered by a healthcare provider in response to an informed patient request), has evolved considerably over the past decade, moving from more personal utility-focused applications outside of traditional healthcare to interfacing with clinical care in nontraditional ways.
