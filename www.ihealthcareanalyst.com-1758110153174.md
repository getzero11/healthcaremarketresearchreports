## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Minimal residual disease (MRD) diagnostics are implemented in most clinical protocols for patients. Testing for measurable or minimal residual disease in people with leukemias and other hematological cancers has gained popularity in the recent years. In addition to stratification for the risk of cancer recurrence, MRD-testing is used to inform transplant-related medical decisions.
