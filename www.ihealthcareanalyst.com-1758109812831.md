## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Medical ablation involves removal of a part of biological tissue or aberrant tissue from within the body via minimally invasive procedures, usually by using percutaneous chemical ablation, radiofrequency ablation, irreversible electroporation, cryoablation, microwave ablation, high-intensity–focused ultrasound ablation equipment.

The global ablation technologies market report estimates the market size ($million 2023 to 2033) for key market segmented by technology or equipment (cryoablation, electric, light or laser, microwave, radiofrequency, and ultrasound), application (cardiovascular, gynecology, oncology, ophthalmology, pain management, and urology) and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).

By technology, the radiofrequency (RF) segment holds the major market share global ablation technologies market due to its high success rate, wide range of approved applications and low complications. The cardiology and oncology application segment recorded the highest market revenue, and it is likely continuing its dominance in the global ablation technologies market during the forecast period. Cryotherapy and ultrasound technologies are the upcoming technologies with new applications introduced by many companies through extensive research and development activities.
