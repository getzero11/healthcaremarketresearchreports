The AI methods excel at automatically recognizing complex patterns in imaging data and providing quantitative, rather than qualitative, assessments of radiographic characteristics.

Worldwide interest in artificial intelligence applications, including imaging, is high and growing rapidly, fueled by availability of large datasets (“big data”), substantial advances in computing power, and new deep-learning algorithms.
