## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Patient warming devices are primarily used in healthcare settings by physicians and nursing staff for better care of the patients during surgeries, patient transfer, severely injured or diseased patients, anesthesia treated patients, and in acute care settings. Patient warming devices help patients tolerate the unintended loss of body heat by helping them achieve normal body temperature or normothermia, leading to faster recovery. Increasing prevalence of chronic ailments is causing the surge in number of surgeries globally. This would in turn likely to increase the sales for patient warming devices. Neonatal hypothermia, aged individuals, patients suffering from cardiovascular or cancerous tumors and patients undergoing surgery are increasingly in need of advanced patient warming devices. Healthcare facilities such as hospitals, healthcare centers, as well as physicians, and nursing staff demand new and advanced patient warming systems and equipment for better and advanced care of the patients. Increasing demand and public awareness about the benefits of patient warming devices are the major factors driving the growth of the global patient warming devices market.
