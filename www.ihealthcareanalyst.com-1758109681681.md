## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Biomedical refrigerators and freezers are designed to store vaccines and other medical products at a stable temperature to prevent their degradation. For storing sensitive material in a laboratory, pharmacy or medical office, a wide selection of medical refrigerators are available in the market. Accurate and uniform temperature in a refrigerator plays a key role in ensuring the life of vaccines, reagents and other biologicals.

Biomedical refrigerators and freezers are medical devices that are used for storage of biological samples such as blood, blood derivatives, vaccine, medicines, biological reagents, and flammable chemicals. Unlike domestic refrigerators and freezers, biomedical refrigerators and freezers maintain constant temperature of the stored samples. Globally rising demand for blood transfusions, organ transplant, cellular therapies, and biopharmaceuticals are fueling the demand for biomedical refrigerators and freezers market.
