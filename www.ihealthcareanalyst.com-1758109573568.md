## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Within hospitals, surgical operations are carried out in a sterile environment in an operating room (OR) or operating theater or operating suite which consists of operating room integration systems, lights, tables, surgical booms and surgical imaging display systems.

The rising concerns about reduction in healthcare spending and achieving accurate surgical outcomes, healthcare providers across the globe are focusing on enhancing operating room efficiency, while minimizing the required resources and related costs.

Some of the factors that are expected to drive market growth during the forecast period include gradual increase in number of ambulatory surgical centers worldwide, increasing demand for hybrid operating rooms and technological advancement in safe and cost-effective operating procedures.
