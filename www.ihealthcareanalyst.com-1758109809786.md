## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Home rehabilitation involves home-based health care facility for substance abuse, mental illness, or other behavioral problems to improve or enhance patients comfort and independence so as to continue recovery at home. Rehabilitation programs help patients to improve, maintain, and restore physical strength and mobility in institutional and home care settings. Home health care devices and services serve prominent alternatives for diagnosis, treatment, and management of these diseases. Chronic diseases such as arthritis, cancer, acquired immunodeficiency syndrome (AIDS), chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), stroke, and respiratory diseases are major health concerns worldwide. Home rehabilitation products assist a set of home rehabilitation services that primarily include physical, occupational, and speech therapy services to help patients recover from acute or chronic injury and other illnesses.
