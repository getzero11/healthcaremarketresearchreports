## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Skincare includes range of practices that support skin integrity including nutrition, avoidance of excessive sun exposure, and appropriate use of emollients; that enhance appearance such as use of cosmetics, botulinum, exfoliation, fillers, laser resurfacing, microdermabrasion, peels, retinol therapy; and that remediate skin break down and relieve skin conditions. Skincare devices are gaining importance not only due to their extensive use in aesthetics but also the increasing incidence of skin disorders worldwide.

These devices enable the efficient diagnosis and treatment of various skin disorders and other indications. The skincare devices used for the diagnosis facilitates the diagnosis of skin lesions suspicious of skin cancers. The treatment devices are based on various technological platforms to treat specific indications and disorders such as cellulite reduction, skin aging, wrinkles, spider veins and vascular lesions.
