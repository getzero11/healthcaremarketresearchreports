## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Chronic disease biological therapies are transitioning from traditional intravenous to subcutaneous administration. Introduction of a wearable device for subcutaneous delivery of larger volume bolus injections encourages patient compliance and reduce the burden on healthcare services. Shifting from inpatient intravenous infusion to outpatient subcutaneous injection not only improves patient safety, treatment adherence, and quality of life but also reduces the total costs of therapy and healthcare-resource utilization.
