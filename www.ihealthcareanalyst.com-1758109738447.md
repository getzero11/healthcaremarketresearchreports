Growing interest in natural horsemanship techniques and products, as well as continued R&D efforts to develop new feed, diagnostics, therapeutics and supplements targeting equine-specific health issues, are additional positive signs that the equine health care market is likely to flourish in the near future. The U.S. and the Middle East, particularly the racing nations of the U.A.E. and other Gulf States are proving to be successful targets for exporters of racing, endurance and healthcare related products such as feed, supplements, diagnostics and therapeutics.
