## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Monitoring a patient’s respiratory status usually takes place in a hospital setting and may be the primary purpose for a patient being observed or admitted to a medical setting. The physical signs of respiratory distress may present as a patient appearing short of breath, having an increased work of breathing, use of their accessory muscles, and changes in skin color, general pallor, or partial or complete loss of consciousness. Technological advancement, rising respiratory disorders and geriatric population worldwide are the major factors driving the growth of the global respiratory monitoring devices market.
