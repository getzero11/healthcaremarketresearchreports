## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

The technology of organ preservation has improved considerably and being constantly modified to provide improved organ storage and outcomes. Several organ-preservation solutions are available and are currently used for kidney, liver, pancreas, small-bowel, lung, and heart transplantation. These solutions provide an artificial medium to the organs that help maintain their survival duration outside the human body before transplantation into a patient.

Technologically advanced solutions, rise in the number of elderly people and presence of effective reimbursement policies are the major factors driving the growth of the global organ preservation solutions market.

The global organ preservation solutions market segmentation is based on product \[University of Wisconsin (UW) solution (ViaSpan), Histidine-tryptophan-ketoglutarate (HTK) solution, Perfadex (PER) , Renograf, HypoThermosol, Lifor, Collins solution \[Euro-Collins (EC)\], Celsior (CEL) solution, others\]; by preservation technique \[static cold storage (SCS), hypothermic perfusion preservation (HPP), others\]; and by organ type \[kidney, liver, heart, lung, others (pancreas & small bowel)\]. Other preservation solutions include Ross-Marshall Citrate solution, Phosphate-Buffered Sucrose solution, TranSend, etc. Other preservation techniques include normothermic machine perfusion, oxygen persufflation, etc.
