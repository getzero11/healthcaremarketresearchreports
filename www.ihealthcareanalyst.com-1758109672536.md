## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Dental consumables or materials are specially fabricated and designed for use in dentistry. There are many different types of dental consumable materials, and their characteristics vary according to their intended purpose. Dental consumables are products which are used on patients in order to treat dental impairments, for tooth restoration and in treatment of associated gingival tissues. For example, temporary dressings, dental restorations (fillings, crowns, bridges), endodontic materials (used in root canal therapy), impression materials, prosthetic materials (dentures), dental implants, and many others.

Dental tourism is a collateral service provided to dental patients along with cost-effective oral treatment in developing economies such as India, Turkey and Hungary. At the same time, the dental reforms and treatment packages introduced by various governments worldwide are likely to positively impact the global dental consumables market.
