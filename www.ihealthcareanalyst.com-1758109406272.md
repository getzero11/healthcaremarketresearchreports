The global mobile medical devices market report provides market size ($Million 2023 to 2033), market share and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).

Mobile medical devices or mobile health devices are software programs that run on smartphones and other mobile communication devices. They can also be accessories that attach to a smartphone or other mobile communication devices, or a combination of accessories and software. Mobile medical devices are created to measure vital signs such as heart rate, blood glucose level or brain activities, process photos of the patient’s skin for dermatological analysis, to help diabetic patients manage their daily routine by visualizing patterns in their blood sugar curve, and even monitor medication compliance by collecting physiological data from a sensor that the patient swallows.

Mobile medical apps are medical devices that are mobile apps, meet the definition of a medical device and are an accessory to a regulated medical device or transform a mobile platform into a regulated medical device. The global mobile medical devices market segmentation is based on product types (blood pressure monitors, cardiac monitors, glucose monitors, multi-parameter monitors, pulse oximeters, sleep apnea monitors).
