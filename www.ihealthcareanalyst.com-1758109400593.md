## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Machine-to-machine (M2M) healthcare solutions include use of medical devices and communication technology, either wired or wireless to monitor diseases and symptoms. Machine-to-machine (M2M) in healthcare refers to technologies that allow both wireless and wired medical devices or equipment to communicate via wired line, Wi-Fi, LAN, Bluetooth, RFID, and other technologies Continuous or even infrequent recording of biomedical indicators is important for the diagnosis, detection and medication of cardiovascular ailments by utilizing remote wearable sensors. Similarly, persistent recording of an electrocardiogram (ECG) or photoplethysmogram (PPG) by a wearable sensor could provide a sensible perspective of the heart state of a patient throughout the day to record conditions as hypertension, anxiety, uneasiness, diabetes, etc. Continuous growth of the geriatric population worldwide and chronic diseases where first responder connectivity plays a vital role, M2M is emerging as a medium for assisted living and a time-saving factor for most healthcare providers around the globe.
