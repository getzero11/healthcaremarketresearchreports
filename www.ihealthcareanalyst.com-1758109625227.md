## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Pharmacovigilance (PV or PhV), also known as drug safety, is science and activities relating to the collection, detection, assessment, understanding, monitoring, and prevention of adverse effects or any other drug-related problem.  Pharmacovigilance activities are performed across all phases of drug development, including preclinical studies, phase I to phase III trials, and phase IV or post market surveillance stages. Information received from patients and healthcare providers, as well as other sources such as the medical literature, plays a critical role in providing the data necessary for Pharmacovigilance. It is more concerned with identifying the hazards associated with pharmaceutical products and with minimizing the risk of any harm that may come to patients.
