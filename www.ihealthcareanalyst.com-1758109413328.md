## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Healthcare business intelligence is the use and analysis of data captured in the healthcare setting to directly inform decision-making that positively impacts patient care delivery, health outcomes and business operations. It refers to broad category of analytics, data warehousing and visualization tools. Healthcare data analytics software gives healthcare companies and hospitals an easy way to prepare, analyze & visualize big and disparate data. The warehouse data could be gathered from multiple source systems, either internal systems, such as electronic health records (EHR) systems, costing or financial systems, or patient satisfaction systems; or external systems, such as systems associated with a state or federal government (e.g., mortality data or cancer registries).
