## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Currently, healthcare industry has much larger demand for cybersecurity software as, data generated from healthcare facilities is vulnerable to virus attacks. Available healthcare cybersecurity software is tailored according to the organization’s specific requirements. Despite of numerous technological developments, healthcare cybersecurity industry is still in developing phases.

There has been an increasing trend of an increasing number of cyber-attacks to the health sector. A lack of adequate IT spending by healthcare organizations and a lack of awareness about cybercrime have exposed the vulnerabilities of healthcare organizations. The overall impact of cyber-attacks on hospitals and healthcare systems is estimated to be nearly six billion per year.

Cyber-attacks are constantly increasing. These kinds of attacks focus mainly on stealing financial information, billing information, and bank account numbers using stolen devices with un-encrypted data, phishing and spam mails. Technological advancements have led to advanced cyber warfare using SQL injections, advanced persistent threats (APT), zero-day attacks, and advanced malware.

Moreover, health organizations face specific threats due to factors such as the use of cloud services, unsecure networks, employee negligence, bring your own device (BYOD) policies, lack of internal identification and security systems, stolen devices with un-encrypted files and others.
