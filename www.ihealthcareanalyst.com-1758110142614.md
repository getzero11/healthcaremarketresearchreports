## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

The contract development and manufacturing organization (CDMO) provides comprehensive services to pharmaceutical and biotechnology firms, covering the entire lifecycle of drug development and manufacturing.  Biologics and advanced therapies represent the forefront of modern medical treatments to address complex diseases.

Protein-based biologics include recombinant proteins, enzymes, antibodies, peptides, and protein-based vaccines. Non-protein vaccines, such as viral vectors and mRNA vaccines, have revolutionized the immunization. Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products (ATMPs) encompass a range of innovative treatments, including non-viral vector-based gene therapies, gene-modified cell therapies to repair or replace damaged tissues and treat genetic disorders.
