## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Oxygen therapy is used for short-term hospitalized patients and as long-term therapy in patients with chronic lung disease which enables patients to remain mobile and active despite their permanent need of oxygen supply and assure all the time sufficient quantity of gas. The medical treatment can be used in both chronic and acute cases either in hospital, pre-hospital or entirely out of hospital. The wider applications of oxygen therapy devices include treatment and management of COPD, asthma, obstructive sleep apnea, pneumonia, and cystic fibrosis.
