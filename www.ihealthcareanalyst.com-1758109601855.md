Phosphodiesterase-5 (PDE-5) inhibitors are one of the most widely used and effective types of medication for treating erectile dysfunction. The first line of treatment for ED comprises oral medication of PDE 5 inhibitors, namely, sildenafil citrate, tadalafil, vardenafil, udenafil and others.
