## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Animal blood products are employed in a variety of laboratory procedures, including their addition to culture media for added enrichment and determination of hemolytic reactions of microorganisms. Animal blood products also serve as sources of stabilized, washed, pooled red blood cells for use in serological procedures. The animal blood types frequently used in the production of culture media are sheep, horse, rabbit and bovine blood.
