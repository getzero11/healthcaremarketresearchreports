Dental laboratories manufacture or customize a variety of products to assist in the provision of oral health care by a licensed dentist. Some of the most common restorations manufactured include crowns, bridges, dentures, and dental implants. Dental laboratories manufacture or customize a variety of products to assist in the provision of oral health care by a licensed dentist, whereas the dental lab technicians follow a prescription from a dentist to manufacture products such as prosthetics (e.g. denture teeth and implants) and therapeutic devices (e.g. orthodontic devices). The global dental laboratories market report primarily focuses on the market as service charges offered by dental laboratories to dentists for development and supply of products such as crowns & bridges, ceramics, veneers, dentures (partial/complete), implants, and orthodontics.
