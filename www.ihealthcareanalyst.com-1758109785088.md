## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Biometric systems in healthcare brings security and convenience to electronic health records of patients, and only medical practitioners can access them quickly, and when required. the biometrics industry has undergone tremendous technological development across all businesses, and it is expected that hospitals, clinics, and other medical and healthcare facilities will be benefited from the increasing scope of applications of biometric security technologies. Doctors, nurses and administrators use biometrics to access EHR records and to provide compliant two-factor authentication for electronic prescription of controlled substances. Additionally, the technologies such as AFIS, fingerprint, IRIS, digital signature, and voice recognition can be used to provide secure access to thousands of electronic medical records in end users like hospitals, defense, banking, consumer health care, and governments throughout the world.

The global healthcare biometric systems market report estimates the market size ($million 2023 to 2033) and segmented based on technology (face recognition, fingerprint identification system (AFIS), hand geometry recognition, iris recognition, signature recognition, vein recognition, voice recognition, other systems); application (logical access control, physical access control, transaction authentication), and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).
