## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Collagen is the primary structural and most important protein of conjunctive tissues and play a key role in making these fundamental components of the human body such as mobile joints, stable bones, smooth skin, glossy hair and healthy fingernails, and thereby ensures the cohesion, elasticity and regeneration of all connective tissues including skin, cartilage and bones.

Collagen peptide is one of the key components of skin that maintains elasticity and hydration. Collagen peptide is manufactured through an enzymatic hydrolysis process and transformed by cosmeceutical and nutraceutical industries into collagen creams, injectables, capsules, powder supplements, drinks and others.
