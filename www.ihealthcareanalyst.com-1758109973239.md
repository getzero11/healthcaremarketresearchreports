## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

The healthcare data platform (HDP) is very similar to a customer data platform in that a variety of different information across the healthcare continuum is automatically collected, securely stored, and easily accessed from one centralized location. The health data platform supports research and analysis both within the health sector and in academia, by allowing access to healthcare data from multiple sources to be brought together as required (on- demand), linked, de-identified and analyzed on a single secure platform.

The global healthcare data platform market research report provides market size ($million 2023 to 2033), market share analysis, growth trends and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).
