## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Cord blood banking facilities stores umbilical cord blood for future use in response to the potential for cord blood in treating diseases of the blood and immune systems. Cord blood banking is practiced for using the cord blood stem cells for the purpose or re-populating blood and treating various medical conditions. Both private and public cord blood banks have developed.

Cord blood stem cells are blood cell progenitors which can form red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets and are currently used to treat blood and immune system related genetic diseases, cancers, and blood disorders.
