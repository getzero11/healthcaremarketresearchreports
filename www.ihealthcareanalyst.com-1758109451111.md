## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Radiofrequency Identification (RFID) smart cabinet are used for the real-time tracking of critical supplies used in health care and medical departments. The RFID storage units are specifically designed to enable hospitals to improve cost containment, patient safety and regulatory compliance.

RFID-enabled solutions to manage supply chains are also useful after products enter inventory at a hospital. RFID smart cabinets create real-time visibility of the tagged items within them, which enables hospitals to track their inventories of expensive drugs, medical devices and other assets.

Although, RFID-enabled smart cabinets are much more expensive than other cabinets hospitals are willing to pay for these products to ensure patient safety, to improve billing efficiency and inventory management, and to minimize the time-consuming, error-prone manual labor involved in documenting inventory.

The integration and deployment of RFID intelligent systems is helping healthcare providers to offer better patient care and automating regulatory compliance by optimizing work flow, offering more efficient services, automation of temperature control and records and ensuring that the correct medical supplies needed for surgeries are ready and waiting in the operating rooms and Patient Care areas.
