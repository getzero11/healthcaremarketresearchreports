## Report Specifications: Market Size, Market Share, Growth Trends, Forecast, Geography

Chromatography systems include a set of laboratory techniques for the separation of mixtures through solid and stationary phases. Preparative chromatography is used to separate the components of a mixture for more advanced use (and is thus a form of purification), whereas analytical chromatography is normally used with smaller amounts of material and is for measuring the relative proportions of analytes in a mixture, and the two are not mutually exclusive. Increasing research and development activities constantly demand the use of chromatography systems. The increasing innovations in instruments and consumables would likely to drive the growth of the global chromatography systems market during the forecast period.
