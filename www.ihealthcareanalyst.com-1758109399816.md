The global patient engagement solutions market report provides market size ($Million 2023 to 2033), market share and forecast (CAGR%, 2025 to 2033).

Today’s physicians are expected to educate their patients, engage them in their own healthcare, and collaborate with them on effective treatments. Patient engagement solutions are a key piece of mobile health technology. Patient engagement is a goal of major health care reform initiatives including Meaningful Use, Patient-Centered Medical Homes (PCMH) and Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs). Patient engagement solution includes patient education resources – from videos to decision aids, an intuitive interface that easily integrates with your EMR, secure, online message center for patients, and searchable resource for the entire community.

The global patient engagement solutions market segmentation is based on product types (application managed services, self-hosted, software as a service), and end users (healthcare payers, hospitals and providers, pharmaceutical companies).
